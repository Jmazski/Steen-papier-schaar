How do you play Rock Paper Scissors?

  • You choose rock, paper or scissors by clicking a button.
  • The computer also randomly picks one of the three.
  • Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, paper beats rock.
  • If you and the computer choose the same, it is a draw.
  • Play just for fun, or up to 10 points!
